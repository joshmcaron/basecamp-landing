Basecamp OS

The intelligent training system for serious mountain athletes.

Basecamp OS is built by someone who lives the alpine training life. It’s not just another fitness app — it’s a personal, adaptive performance system for anyone training toward Denali, Everest, UTMB, and other summit-level goals.

Whether you're running, strength training, or using a hypoxic system, Basecamp adapts daily based on your effort, time, and gear. It includes an AI trainer, workout fallback logic, recovery sliders, and a goal-specific UI.

The app is currently available for Android through the Google Play Store. iOS support is planned.

From Denali to the Himalayas — this is your basecamp.
